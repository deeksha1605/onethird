Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the price?

Fully furnished apartment including appliances FREE Wifi internet Complimentary Toiletries (Shampoo, Body Soap, Tooth Brush) 12 hour reception, 7 days a week Housekeeping is provided for bookings of 5 nights or more Your apartment is fully self-contained and includes linen, towels, hair dryer. Most of our apartments are equipped with a kitchen including a microwave, fridge, cooktop, kettle and washing machine with dryer. Note towels will not be changed unless housekeeping is scheduled for your apartment, feel free to use the washing machine to freshen them up if required. Please refer to our each apartments description for a list of inclusions.

Can we request bedding configuration?

Bedding configuration is pre-determined. A maximum of 1 extra bedding is available as a Japanese futon set and charged at 3,000JPY per night. Limited baby cots are also available at the same pricing of an extra bed, please contact us early to avoid disappointment.

Does my apartment have an elevator access?

Elevator access may be available depending on the apartments selected. Not all our apartments are in buildings with an elevator. Please refer to each apartments description for a list of inclusions.

I have booked multiple apartments, are they all in the same building?

We have different type of apartments located in different buildings. If you have booked in the same area but different type of apartments, please contact us to confirm locations.

Do your apartment have Wifi internet?

All our apartments are connected with high-speed Wi-Fi access. Unlimited data usage and no restriction on the number of devices.

Is there a car park available?

Unfortunately, we do not have parking facilities. There are many public parking facilities available near our apartments. Please confirm the terms and conditions with the parking operator.

How do I book?

Book online for the best available rates. Alternatively call us on +81 50 3136 5165.

Is a deposit required?

A 10% deposit will be taken automatically at time of booking to secure your reservation, this will be deducted out of your outstanding accommodation fee.

How do I pay for my booking?

We accept payment by cash or credit card upon arrival.The following credit cards can be used for payment: VISA, MasterCard, American Express and JCB.Pre-payment via Paypal and Direct Debit is also available. Please contact us on +81 50 3136 5165

Is a credit card required?

Yes – a credit card must be presented on check-in, a copy will be taken as guarantee. If a credit card can not be provided a security bond of 20,000JPY is required upon check-in. The security bond is refunded on departure subject to verification of no damage to the apartment or any outstanding charges.

What is the cancellation policy?

Your deposit will be refunded in full if cancelled more than 7 days before check-in. Cancellation within 7 days will incur 100% charge of the full accommodation cost.

How do I cancel my reservation?

Call us on +81 50 3136 5165 or email info@1-3rd.com for any cancellations. Your deposit will be refunded in full if cancelled more than 7 days before check-in via the original method of payment.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in time is 3pm. Early check-in requests are subject to availability upon arrival. If you require your apartment to be available prior to our 3pm check-in, we suggest you book the night prior to your arrival date to ensure the apartment is ready for your arrival. Check-out time is 10am. Late checkouts are not available and failure to vacate your apartment at the designated time might incur a late fee.

Where can I store my luggage?

You are welcome to store your luggage before check-in time at Akihabara Office, Tokyo, Chiyoda-ku, Sotokanda 4-6-10. We do not offer luggage storage after check-out.

Can I receive mail/parcel or luggage at your serviced apartments?

You are welcome to get mail or luggages delivered to our main office address if the arrival date is within your period of stay. Guests are expected to collect any mail received on their behalf. If a package arrives after your departure, you will have to organise for it to be forwarded to you. Please address deliveries to : 1/3rd Residence Group Tokyo, Chiyoda-ku, Sotokanda 4-6-10 2nd Floor 101-0021 Japan

How far are the serviced apartments from the airports?

1/3rd Residence Serviced Apartments Main Office is located at Tokyo, Chiyoda-ku, Sotokanda 4-6-10 and approximately 40 minutes trip from Haneda Airport or 60 minutes trip from Narita Airport by train. It costs about 10,000JPY (including tolls) one way for a Taxi to Haneda Airport.Our Yoyogi Offices are approximately 40min from Haneda Airport by train and bus or 1h15min from Narita Aiport by train. A one way taxi to Haneda Airport will cost around 12,000JPY from the Yoyogi area. Airport Limousine bus service is also available for both Haneda and Narita airport, see the following link for timetables and pricing www.limousinebus.co.jp/en/
